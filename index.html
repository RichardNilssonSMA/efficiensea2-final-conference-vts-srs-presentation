  • Well liked by sailors and VTS Operators alike

How reports are presented in VTS Admin
  • Nice presentation of reports and reports easily accessible and read
  • Badges are used to highlight important information
  • Badges color coded using same colors as are used internally at Sound VTS
  • Next up: processed feature of VTS Admin
Using the processed feature in VTS Admin
  • This feature is used to exclude reports that have been processed from the list of reports
  • The processed reports are sent to the bottom and stay there unless they are marked as unprocessed
  • Even though they have been marked as processed they can still be opened with ease
  • When marked as unprocessed the reports will immediately appear in the list again, sorted correctly
